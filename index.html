Front-End Developer/Designer

About

I'm a self taught Front-end developer/designer from Germany. I started my education with Free Code Camp in February 2017 and have since then done two internships.
My skills include:

  • HTML/CSS
  • Javascript
  • jQuery
  • Node.js
  • React.js

I'm always open to new ideas and like to learn new stuff.
I create designs that are unusual but still follow common usability conventions. Websites should be fun to look at and make you want to explore it.
But I pay even closer attention to making them highly usable and avoiding any unnecessary clutter.
